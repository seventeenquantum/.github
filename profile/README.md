# 17 Quantum

> Africa-first quantum technology company.

We research, build, and deploy quantum technologies — from hardware and algorithms to products and education — to ensure Africa leads in the quantum era, not just participates in it.

---

## What We Build

| Vertical | Description |
|---|---|
| **Research & Development** | Foundational and applied quantum research targeting real-world problems in healthcare, energy, and agriculture across Africa |
| **Hardware** | Design and development of quantum hardware systems and components |
| **Algorithm Development** | Quantum algorithms engineered for African-context problem domains |
| **Products** | Software products — web platforms, mobile apps, and developer tools — built on quantum and classical infrastructure |
| **Education** | Africa's quantum computing education platform, providing free and low-cost access to structured courses and real quantum hardware |

## This Organisation

This GitHub organisation is home to all 17 Quantum engineering work — research codebases, hardware tooling, algorithm libraries, product repositories, and open course material.

**Repository visibility policy:**

- All repositories are **private by default**
- Repositories are made **public intentionally** — open-source releases, course material, and published research
- Public repositories are clearly scoped in their own `README.md`

---

## Open Repositories

### Education
Course repositories follow the naming convention `course-[technology]-[topic]-[level]` and are open to all learners.

Each course repo has two branches:

| Branch | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `main` | Starter code and exercises — fork this to begin |
| `solution` | Completed reference implementation — consult after submission |

> Browse all course repos by filtering this org for the `course-` prefix.
